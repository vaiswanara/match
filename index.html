What is Ashtakuta Matching?

In Hindu astrology, before a marriage is arranged, the horoscopes of the bride and groom are compared using 8 compatibility factors called Kutas. Together they add up to a maximum of 36 points.

📌 General Rule A score of 18 or more out of 36 is considered acceptable. Scores above 27 are excellent. Below 18 is generally not recommended by traditional astrologers.
